Getting Your Thoughts Out There

A new way to share the discussion among your group.

  1. Sign Up for a Prose.io account
  2. Get a Github account
  3. Connect your account to your Github repo.
  4. Open the repo folder which you are asked to post to.
  5. Begin a new file, enter your content.
  6. Commit your content by naming in the file name bar and click save.
