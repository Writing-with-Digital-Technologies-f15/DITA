  1. Login to GitHub.
  2. Authorize startup of Prose.io.
  3. Select "responses" repository.
  4. Select subfolder for appropriate week.
  5. Click on the green box that says "new file".
  6. Select text to be posted from the word processor on your computer.
  7. Paste text in place of "A New Post" heading.
  8. Select filename path at the top of the page.
  9. Add your name to the file name.
  10. Click disk icon on the right side of the page.
  11. Describe the changes you made in the box that appears.
  12. Click commit.
